About The Hayle Group

The Hayle Group LLC is an NMSDC-Certified and New Jersey Certified Minority Business Enterprise (MBE) safety consulting firm specializing in transit, infrastructure, and public-sector construction safety oversight.

With 17+ years of safety experience on transit and infrastructure projects, the firm brings direct, hands-on experience within MTA/NYCT, LIRR, NJ Transit, Amtrak, and NYS DOT operating environments — including full responsibility for safety oversight, compliance enforcement, Safe Work Plan review and approval, and incident reporting in high-risk, active transit conditions.

The Hayle Group is a principal-led firm providing independent construction safety consulting and compliance services to owners, public agencies, construction managers, design-builders, and contractors throughout New York and New Jersey, with direct oversight and accountability on every engagement.

Available for: Owner’s Representative, General Contractor, Design-Builder, Emergency Response, and Short-Term Safety Coverage throughout New York and New Jersey.
